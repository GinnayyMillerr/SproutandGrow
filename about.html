About Sprout & Grow

Sprout & Grow was founded in Raleigh, NC with one simple mission: to help people grow beautiful, sustainable gardens. Our team believes that gardening can transform not just landscapes, but lives and communities too.

We are committed to offering high-quality organic plants, providing expert gardening advice, and encouraging eco-friendly practices that benefit both people and the planet.

Sprout & Grow is more than a nursery — it’s a community hub where knowledge blossoms, friendships grow, and green thumbs are nurtured!

Meet the Owner!

Hi, I'm Lily, the proud owner of Sprout & Grow! Gardening has been my passion since childhood, and I’m dedicated to helping others discover the joy of growing organically. My mission is to make sustainable gardening easy, beautiful, and rewarding for everyone — whether you have a big backyard or a cozy city balcony. I'm so excited to grow a greener community together with you!
